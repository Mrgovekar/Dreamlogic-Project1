Toddle is a venture-backed teaching and learning solution for IB and similar schools. Accessibility is a key requirement for them to ensure a seamless experience. Incskill worked on an audit, provided contextual developer assistance, helped with responding to contractual requirements and continues to be involved ultimately leading to a voluntary product accessibility template (VPAT).

About Incskill

Founded by people with disabilities with lived experience and decade's of accessibility, engineering and AI experience, Incskill strongly believes in the power of technology to enable developers and content creators to develop more accessible internet for people with disabilities.

Our Vision

E-learning has opened up massive opportunities to develop skills and find meaningful career opportunities. People from underrepresented communities and non-traditional learners have not been able to take equal advantage due to inaccessibility of content, interface and pedagogy. We are on a mission to help these students access these resources and content on an equal basis in a format, style and community that works for them, thereby helping them develop 21st-century skills and become employable.
